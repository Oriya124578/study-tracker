The 30% rule
~70% of any screen is warm cream + white + ink. ~30% is green (FAB, italic accents, CTA, meals). Any other color is information — appears only on item-type cards or stat values. Never decorative.

Italic accent rule
Every Instrument Serif heading contains one or two italic words in green — the editorial pull-quote effect. Names, day-of-week, key nouns, the seconds of a timer. Never italicize body text or labels.
